[SECTION 4] ORDERING & CHECKOUT FLOW
================================================================================

Step-by-Step Ordering Process:

1. BROWSE MENU
   - Browse by category: All, Coffee, Non Coffee, Snack, Dessert
   - Use search bar to find specific items
   - Select size (if available) and quantity
   - Click "Add to cart"

2. REVIEW CART (Checkout Step 1)
   - View all added items in the right sidebar cart
   - Adjust quantities (+/−) or remove items
   - Select fulfillment type: Dine-in, Delivery, or Take-away
   - See subtotal, shipping fee, and tax breakdown
   - Click "Continue to Checkout"

3. CUSTOMER INFO (Checkout Step 2)
   - Fill in required fields:
     * Full Name (required)
     * Phone Number (required)
     * Age (optional)
     * Email (optional)
     * Delivery Address (required for Delivery orders)
   - Click "Place Order" to confirm

4. ORDER CONFIRMATION
   - Order is placed and appears in "My Orders" section
   - Real-time tracking available in "Order Tracking" section

Fulfillment Types:
  - Dine-in: No shipping fee. Enjoy your order in-store.
  - Delivery: 15.000 VND shipping fee. Delivered to your address.
  - Take-away: No shipping fee. Pick up at counter.

Tax Rate: 15% applied on subtotal.

[SECTION 7] REFUND & CANCELLATION POLICIES
================================================================================

Order Cancellation:
  - Orders can be canceled BEFORE the rider picks up the order
  - To cancel: Go to "My Orders" → Select active order → Click "Cancel Order"
  - A cancellation confirmation dialog will appear for safety
  - Canceled orders cannot be reinstated — please reorder if needed

Refund Policy:
  - Online payment: Refund processed automatically to wallet/bank within 24 hours
  - Cash payment: Collect refund at the Cashier Counter in-store
  - Partial refunds are not available — full order amount will be refunded

Re-ordering:
  - Previously completed orders can be reordered from "My Orders" → History tab
  - Click "Reorder" to add all items from a past order back to your cart

[SECTION 8] FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS (FAQs)
================================================================================

Q1: What is the refund policy for canceled orders?
A1: Refunds will be automatically processed to your wallet/bank account within 24 hours for online payments. For cash payments, please collect your refund directly at the Cashier Counter.

Q2: How can I change my delivery address?
A2: Changes are only supported before the rider picks up the order. You can go to 'My Orders' to cancel the current order and reorder with a new address. Or update your default address in Settings.

Q3: How many addresses can I save in the Address Book?
A3: You can save up to 5 different addresses in your Address Book. Remember to set one as 'Default' to auto-fill at Checkout.

Q4: How do I contact customer support?
A4: You can call our Hotline at 1900 1234 (toll-free), send a message via Zalo, or Messenger. Support hours: 07:00 — 22:00 every day.

Q5: What are your operating hours?
A5: We are open from 07:00 AM to 10:00 PM every day, including weekends and public holidays.

Q6: Do you offer free delivery?
A6: Yes! Orders over 200.000 VND qualify for free delivery. Otherwise, a flat delivery fee of 15.000 VND applies.

Q7: Can I customize my drink?
A7: Currently, you can choose between Small, Medium, and Large sizes for eligible drinks. Additional customizations (sugar level, ice level, extra shots) will be available in a future update.

Q8: How long does delivery take?
A8: Estimated delivery time is 15–30 minutes depending on your distance from our store.

Q9: What if my order arrives damaged or incorrect?
A9: Please contact our support team immediately via Hotline (1900 1234) or Zalo with a photo of the issue. We will arrange a replacement or full refund within 24 hours.

Q10: Can I schedule an order for later?
A10: Scheduled ordering is not available yet. Currently, all orders are placed for immediate preparation. This feature is planned for a future update.

Q11: Is there a loyalty or rewards program?
A11: We are currently developing a loyalty program where you will earn points for every purchase. Stay tuned for updates! Expected launch: Q3 2026.

Q12: Can I order for someone else (gift order)?
A12: Yes! During checkout, simply enter the recipient's name, phone number, and delivery address in the customer info form. You can add a note for the rider if needed.

Q13: What if I forgot to apply a promo code?
A13: Unfortunately, promo codes cannot be applied after an order has been placed. Please make sure to enter any promo code before confirming your order. Contact support if you need assistance.

Q14: Do you offer catering or bulk orders?
A14: Yes, we accept bulk orders for events and corporate catering. Please contact us directly via Hotline or email at hello@matikocoffee.com at least 48 hours in advance.

Q15: Are there any allergen warnings for your menu items?
A15: Our products may contain common allergens including milk, nuts, wheat (gluten), and soy. If you have specific allergies, please contact our support team before ordering. Allergen information for each product will be available in a future menu update.
